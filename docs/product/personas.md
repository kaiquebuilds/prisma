# Persona 1: Alex (The Planner)

- **Name:** Alex
- **Age:** 32
- **Profession:** Software Engineer
- **Bio:** Alex is a successful engineer with a stable, comfortable salary. He is tech-savvy and understands the importance of financial planning. However, he lacks the tools to get a clear, consolidated view of his finances. He uses multiple credit cards and bank accounts, and while he isn't in debt, he feels he isn't saving as effectively as he should be. He wants to optimize his finances to build long-term wealth.
- **Goals:**
  - Understand where his money is truly going each month.
  - Optimize his budget to increase his savings rate.
  - Plan for long-term goals like buying a property and investing.
- **Pain Points:**
  - "I make a good salary, but I don't know where it all goes."
  - "My bank's app is clunky and only shows me part of the picture."
  - "I want to see my net worth and investment progress in one place."

# Persona 2: Juliana (The Overwhelmed)

- **Name:** Juliana
- **Age:** 45
- **Profession:** Nurse, Public Hospital
- **Bio:** Juliana is a dedicated nurse who feels perpetually trapped in a cycle of debt. She raised two children as a single mother and now works every Saturday just to cover her expenses. She is not tech-savvy and has given up on using spreadsheets. She tries to keep track of upcoming bills in her head, which causes immense stress and leads to her occasionally forgetting one. She is exhausted and feels that no matter how hard she works, she will never get ahead.
- **Goals:**
  - Pay off her existing loans and break the debt cycle.
  - Stop losing sleep over upcoming bills.
  - Go back to having her weekends free.
- **Pain Points:**
  - "I'm so tired of working every single weekend just to survive."
  - "I'm always worried I've forgotten to pay a bill."
  - "No matter how much I earn, I know that I'll spend everything."
  - "Financial apps and spreadsheets are too complicated for me."
